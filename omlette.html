Breakfast Omlette

Description

How to make Naim's breakfast omlette using the ingredients and instructions below:

Ingredients
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 Mushrooms
  • Half cup of spinach
  • 1 teaspoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Optional: Medium Cheddar Cheese
Instructions
  1. Bring a pan to low heat and add 1 teaspoon of extra virgin olive oil and 1 teaspoon of garlic powder.
  2. Slice 3 Mushrooms and add into pan.
  3. Mix 2 eggs in a bowl with 1 teaspoon of salt.
  4. Flip mushrooms after turning to golden brown. Then add the half cup of spinach and eggs into pan covering with lid
  5. Cook the eggs for 3-5 minutes until solid. Flip over half the omlette. *Optional: Add shredded cheese before flipping*
