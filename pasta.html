Naim's Secret Pasta

Description

How to make Naim's Secret Pasta to perfection using the ingredients and instructions below

Ingredients:
  • Rigotoni Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell Peppers
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Spinach
  • Shalot
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Butter
  • Medium Aged Cheddar Cheese
Instructions:
  1. Boil water with a sprinkle of salt
  2. Add Rigotoni Pasta into water once boiling begins. Cook for 10-15 minutes.
  3. In a low heated pan, add a tablespoon of butter, salt and black pepper
  4. Grind up one full Garlic and 2 tablespoons worth of ginger. Thinly dice one Shalot. Then add into pan to simmer.
  5. Dice half a cup of cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, and mushrooms (each). Then add into pan for about 5 mins.
  6. Add spinach, one tablespoon of oregano, and full pasta sauce bottle into pan.
  7. Drain pasta and keep 1/5th of a cup of pasta water. Then add both into pan.
  8. Add 3 basil leaves and half a cup of shredded Medium Aged Cheddar into pan.
  9. Cook until cheese is melted and ingredients are mixed together well.
  10. Serve and Enjoy!
